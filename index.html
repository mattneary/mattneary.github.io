My current employment is as a Decision Scientist at a hedge fund. (I make internal software tools.)

In 2019 I graduated from Harvard with bachelor's degree in History & Science

My senior year I wrote a thesis on Ted Nelson's Project Xanadu titled “Xanadu: A Literary Dream for the Computer Age.” Apple cofounder Steve Wozniak called it “incredible.”

Before that, I worked as a Software Engineer at Kensho. Ultimately, we were acquired in what Forbes called the “Largest A.I. Deal in History.”

Past open-source projects of mine include a command prompt screencaster (Mimesis), a Chrome extension for better tab management (command-t), and a book on computation from first principles (Tarpits & Abstraction).
