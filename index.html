In 2020 I graduated from Harvard with an honors degree in History & Science

My senior year I wrote a thesis on Ted Nelson's Project Xanadu titled “Xanadu: A Literary Dream for the Computer Age.” Apple cofounder Steve Wozniak called it “incredible.”
